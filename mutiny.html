Brief Description

Mutiny is a social network developed for a Hackathon contest. It was inspired by the hit TV show "Halt and catch fire".


Our team loved music, books and films so we built a social network where you enjoy a personalized feed of your favorite art enhanced by the ability to communicate your thoughts and converse with your friends on the topics you love. Whether this is a book, a movie or a music album, you can create a post and invite your friends to chat. If one of them declares owenership of a unique collectible then go private with her/him, create a collectible item and buy it. Hustle free! Mutiny is a unified timeline of your favorite artistic web adventures. Come in and mutiny on!
